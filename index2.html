WALL PANELS

Acoustic CNS Panels

Acoustic CNS cut wall panels are precision-cut panels designed to improve sound absorption while adding aesthetic appeal to interior spaces. These panels are typically crafted from high-density materials such as MDF (Medium Density Fiberboard), plywood, or fiber cement boards and feature CNC (Computer Numerical Control) cutting technology to create intricate patterns, perforations, or grooves that enhance their acoustic performance.

Acoustic Screens

Acoustic Screens are soundproof barriers or panels designed to absorb, block, or redirect sound to reduce noise levels and enhance the acoustics of a space. They are often used in environments where noise control is crucial, such as offices, studios, factories, hospitals, or public areas. Acoustic screens come in various shapes, sizes, and materials, offering flexibility in how they can be deployed to manage sound effectively.

Printed Acoustic Panels

Printed Acoustic Panels are a visually appealing and effective solution for controlling sound in a variety of spaces. These panels combine the benefits of sound absorption with custom graphics or artwork, making them a popular choice in environments where both acoustics and aesthetics matter.
